**********
Poor items
**********

.. index:: Poor items managment

Sell Poor items
==================

.. index:: Sell poor items

Sell poor items to current vendor ::

	/mi psell

..

Destroy Poor Items
==================

.. index:: Destroy Poor Items

Destroy poor items ::

	/mi pdestroy

..

.. note:: there's not a confirm prompt, item will be deleted as soon as you launch the command.
